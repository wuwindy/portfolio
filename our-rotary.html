Our Rotary APP

Design Background

This requirement is from the Rotary member to build an app for Rotary communication. It provides the shop to set their information. And also know the meeting time and details.

Role and Collaborations

In this project, I have cooperated with the ios and android Engineer. Discuss with them and the stakeholder about some app flow and design.

Team

UI / Web Designer - Windy Wu
IOS Engineer
Android Engineer

Platform

IOS, Android

Tools / Skill

Sketch, Photoshop, Illustrator

Design Goal

Module design

Let the user choose the different company design theme.

Clear information

Simple design to show the clear information.

Form optimization

Optimize the design to present the meeting information.

Outcome and Impact

Through the app, users can more conveniently know the meeting information.

Enterprises can freely choose module styles for promotion.

This app is used by our rotary members of the Hsinchu. And users have the positive feedback.

User Research

Pain point - doesn't have a convenient app or platform to know the rotary information

Requirement - Need a platform to know the rotary meeting and upload information

Wireframe

Design

Challenge: The layout suffers conflicting complex text information

We realized that attendance information is the most important that users want to know. We use the icons to reduce the information.

Results

We solve the design challenges and realize our users and define the flow. Finally, stakeholders and rotary members have positive feedback. They say can set and understand the meeting information and can choose different type of shop design.

Garmin Women

Design Background

We have got the data information that the Garmin user is 70% of male. And from data mining, we found that the female percentage is still not high growth. Most women think that the Garmin running watches is more function, so it doesn't good to match with the outfit. They more prefer the beautiful watches.
Thus, we want to do this project for brand promotion and expand the new female user.

Low ratio of female users

Brand positioning

Exploit female market

Design Goal

01

Brand promotion

03

Colorful visual

User Research

Age - 26

Pain point - Don't know how to wear the watches to match the fashion outfit

Requirement - Want a beautiful exterior and daily exercise tracking watch

Team Character

Graphic designer and I play the role of photographer. And I also design the layout and coding. We coordinated with the project manager and the key opinion leader to discuss how to let women users know the fun of running.

Design

The project has introduced eight women runner's stories. Used eight gradient colors to representative different runner personalities. The landing page sings a responsive design that supports different form factors and devices.

Results

By visiting the landing page, many users know that the women runner of Garmin. And the project manager provided feedback that some users are attractive want to buy the smartwatch.

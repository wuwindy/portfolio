I am windy wu

I am Windy Wu, a design thinking explorer, and like to travel to the world.

I have many years of UI / UX and Web design. I approach the product with a human-centered design based on product strategy, problem-solving, and user testing. Designing a user-friendly product is my main principle.

Born and raised in Tainan, and I earned my master's degree in interactive design at National Taipei Technology University. I like to travel. Specifically, I enjoy places with a long history and culture to view to cultivate a global vision. And I tried to contribute to making a positive contribution to the world and those around me.

I am windy wu

I am Windy Wu, a design thinking explorer, and like to travel to the world.

Recently, I designed the product page for Garmin, the world's leading smartwatches company, and serving as the web designer for Asia markets. Our team reshaped the product page from cultural differences in Asia markets. I approach the product page projects with human-centered design. The projects are based on product strategy, problem-solving, and user testing. Design a user-friendly web page is my main principle.

Born and raised in Tainan, and I earned my master's degree in interactive design at National Taipei Technology University. I like to travel. Specifically, I enjoy places with a long history and culture to view to cultivate a global vision. And I tried to contribute to making a positive contribution to the world and those around me.

Polisnetwork

Design Background

PolisNetwork's primary goal is to establish a tool for presenting personalized advertisement tracking data. The main target users are small individual blog maintainers who can utilize PolisNetwork's services for advertising placement and tracking.

Role and Collaborations

As the product designer, I discussed with the stakeholder to explore the design style and how to work. And define the brand color and design system.

Team

Product Designer - Windy Wu
Backend Engineer

Platform

Web

Tools / Skill

Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator, After effects , Html, CSS, Jquery

Design Goal

Clear and Concise

Clear and concise content ensures that important information is readily apparent.

Visual Consistency

Maintain visual uniformity by utilizing brand colors, fonts, and logos, among other elements.

Intuitive Navigation

A concise and intuitive navigation structure that allows users to easily find the information they need.

Take away

Through this project, I primarily learned to utilize After Effects and integrate Lottie plugins to generate Canvases that can be used on webpages, thereby achieving better dynamic effects.

The experience of collaborating with engineers was quite positive. Despite the time zone differences, using Git for version control to mutually confirm progress was a rather good experience.

This project also benefited from the early establishment of a Design System, which greatly facilitated communication. As a result, maintaining design consistency throughout the project became much easier.

Blog

Overview

Overall, PolisoNetwork features a minimalist web design complemented by animated scrolling, presenting simple yet vibrant pages. The aim is to provide viewers with a better browsing experience.
